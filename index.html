About Me



I am a Patricia Caldwell Postdoctoral Scholar at Virginia Tech. Prior to that, I was a PhD student at UC Berkeley, supervised by Alexander Givental. From Summer 2018 to Spring 2021 I was an NSF Graduate Student Fellow.

I grew up in Ithaca, NY, and subsequently did my undergraduate degree at Columbia University.
My research interests are in quantum K-theory and related areas, including mirror symmetry, integrable systems, and GLSMs. If you have questions about any of my work, or would like to collaborate on something, don’t hesitate to contact me.
